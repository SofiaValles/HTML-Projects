Gamjajeon

  • potatoes
  • onions
  • soy sauce
  • chilli flakes or fresh chilli pepper
  • sugar
  • rice vinegar (any can be used)
  • salt
  1. First wash and peal the potatoes and onions.
  2. now process or grate the pottatoes and onions.
  3. Let them rest in a strainer for 15 minutes with a bowl underneath to collect the liquids.
  4. Mix the potato starch (the white part in the liquid bowl)and the potato and onion mixture, discarding the liquid, add salt and put them into a pan with like pancakes and cook until golden on each side.
  5. mixt 2 tbsp of soysauce and 1 of vinegar with some chilli flakes and one small table spoon of sugar to make the sauce.
